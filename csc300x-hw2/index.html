Who is Grandma Bettsy?

Grandma Bettsy, a lively and spirited woman, was born and raised in the heart of Louisiana, surrounded by the vibrant culture and rich traditions of the bayou. She hailed from a long line of Cajun ancestors who had settled in the region generations ago, passing down their unique customs and stories.

As a young girl, Bettsy was captivated by the mystical and mysterious nature of the Louisiana bayou. She would often explore the marshy lands, getting lost in the labyrinth of cypress trees and Spanish moss that draped from their branches. It was during one of these adventures that she discovered her love for foraging herbs and plants that were not only essential for Cajun cuisine but also held medicinal properties passed down through generations.
